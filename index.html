Home clocks can also be smart

BYTE CLOCK-smart desktop clock for home or office

A minimalist clock connected to Wi-Fi will complement your interior or workplace.
Bright dials will attract your attention.
They are equipped with a screen that displays - not only the time.

Here are the main functionality of this wonderful device:

  • Display of date, time, and day of the week
  • Display of the ambient air temperature at the installation location
  • Outdoor air temperature display (thanks to the wireless sensor)
  • Display of atmospheric pressure including the dynamics of its changes
  • Congratulations on major holidays (via the ticker effect)
  • Getting statistics (number of followers) of your Instagram account
  • Ability to share your weather data with the service narodmon.ru
  • Time synchronization with google.com
  • The device is equipped with a light sensor (for automatic adjustment of the display brightness)
  • The clock is configured using a convenient Android application

  • The watch is very easy to use, just set it up once with
    a convenient app and it will delight you every day.
    A number of quick settings can be made using the touch sensors
    located on the top of the device

  • The device connects to the smartphone via a Bluetooth connection.
    The application is intuitive and will not cause you any difficulties when using it.
    If you want to connect your device to a WI-FI network to get Instagram statistics
    or send weather data (the temperature value of the additional sensor and atmospheric pressure) to the service narodmon.ru,
    then enter the SSID, PASSWORD, and click OK. Also, turn on the radio buttons to activate the corresponding functions.
    Sending weather data to the service narodmon.ru occurs at intervals of 10 minutes.
    Statistics on the number of Instagram followers are requested every 4 hours.
    You can turn off the light sensor and set the display brightness yourself by turning on the AUTO BRIGHT OFF switch.
    Enabling and disabling the operating modes is shown on the display using information messages.
    Sensors of well-known world manufacturers are used to measure temperature and atmospheric pressure
    Bosch, Maxim Integrated. The temperature measurement range of the optional (wireless) sensor is from -40 to +85 degrees.
    The device settings are stored in its FLASH memory and are not reset when the power is turned off.

  • The optional temperature sensor is a stand-alone device that transmits
    temperature data (with a period of 12 minutes) at the place of its installation via a wireless communication channel using
    using a secure communication protocol. Also, each such sensor has a unique identifier for error-free detection
    by the clock. The communication range can reach up to 15 meters indoors and up to 30 meters in open areas.
    Three AAA batteries are used as the sensor's power source. The low-power mode
    allows the device to operate from one set of batteries for up to 1 year.
